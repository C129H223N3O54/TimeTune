How to use TimeTune

📄

1. Export & Upload

Go to exportify.net, export your Spotify playlist as CSV and drag it into TimeTune.

🎴

2. Flip cards to preview

Click any card to flip it. The front shows the QR code, the back shows artist, title and year.

📱

3. QR Code

The QR code on the front links directly to the song on Spotify. Scan it with your phone during the game to play the song.

✎

4. Custom text per card

Flip a card to the back and click the dashed input field to add individual text — e.g. a category or hint. Use Settings → Custom Text to add the same text to all cards at once.

⚙️

5. Settings

Choose a color scheme, adjust font size and QR code size — the preview updates live. Select duplex or single-sided printing.

🖨️

6. Print & Play

Click Create PDF, print on 200 g/m² cardstock at 100% scale, cut along the dashed lines — and play!

💡 Tip: Use duplex printing so the QR code and the song info are printed on both sides of the same card automatically.
